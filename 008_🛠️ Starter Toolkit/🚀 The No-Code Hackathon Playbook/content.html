Welcome to the fast track! This playbook is for every team that wants to go from a brilliant idea to a working demo in record time. No-code and low-code platforms are your secret weapon in a 3-hour sprint. They let you focus on what truly matters: your creative idea, clever prompt engineering, and the logic of your AI application, not on boilerplate code or complex server setups.Spotlight on bolt.new: Your Hackathon Superpower⭐ Bolt.new! ⭐What is it?Think of Bolt as a visual canvas for building AI workflows. You can drag and drop "blocks" that represent different AI models (like GPT-4o, Claude, or Stable Diffusion), connect them to other web services, and orchestrate complex logic without writing extensive code. It's the perfect tool to build the powerful "backend" for your hackathon project.A Simple Recipe for Success with Bolt:Build a simple UI using Gradio or even just Bolt's built-in input forms.Create a Bolt workflow that takes the user's input from the UI.Send the input to an LLM block (like OpenAI's GPT-4o) with a creative prompt.Process the output and send it back to your UI to display to the user.This simple pattern is the foundation for almost any AI app you can imagine. Other Powerful No-Code ToolsWhile Bolt is our featured tool, the no-code universe is vast! Here are a few other excellent platforms you can use:n8n: A powerful, open-source workflow automation tool. It's fantastic for connecting hundreds of different apps and APIs in a visual, node-based editor.Bubble: The go-to platform if you want to build a full-stack web application with user accounts, databases, and complex front-end interactions, all without code.Voiceflow: The undisputed champion for building advanced AI chatbots and voice assistants. If your idea is conversational, start here.Remember: It's All About the LLM!Whichever platform you choose, remember the core of this hackathon is the creative and intelligent application of Large Language Models (LLMs). Your no-code workflow must connect to an LLM (like GPT, Gemini, or Claude) to perform the central task of your project.Fun & Creative No-Code Project IdeasNeed a spark of inspiration? Here are a few ideas that are perfect for a no-code build:The AI Trip Planner: A simple form asks for a destination, number of days, and travel style (e.g., "adventure," "relaxing," "foodie"). The workflow sends this to an LLM to generate a detailed, day-by-day travel itinerary.The Automated Content Summarizer: Create a workflow that watches a specific RSS feed or website. When a new article is published, it uses an LLM to summarize it and then posts that summary to a Discord or Slack channel.The "Explain It To Me" Engine: A UI with a textbox and a dropdown menu (e.g., "Explain to a 5-year-old," "Explain to a CEO"). The workflow takes the user's complex topic and the target audience, and has an LLM generate a tailored explanation.The Personalized Workout Generator: A form asks for your fitness goals, available equipment, and time. The workflow sends this to an LLM with a prompt to act as a personal trainer and create a custom workout plan.
