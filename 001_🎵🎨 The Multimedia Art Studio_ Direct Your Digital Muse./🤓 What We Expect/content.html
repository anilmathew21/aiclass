For this hackathon, your goal is to build a functional prototype of your AI Art Studio. We're looking for a seamless, magical experience that transforms a user's idea into a multi-sensory "scene." A successful project should:1. Accept a Rich Creative Prompt:Your UI must accept a descriptive prompt for a scene, not just a mood. Think bigger! For example: "a rainy, neon-lit street in Tokyo," or "a peaceful, sun-drenched meadow at dawn."2. Generate a Cohesive Multimedia Triptych:The Soundtrack: Generate an original instrumental track using a music API or model that captures the core emotion of the scene.The Narrative: Generate a matching poem, story snippet, or internal monologue using an LLM that brings the scene's story to life.The Visual: Generate a key piece of "concept art" or a "keyframe" for the scene using an image generation model, providing a stunning visual anchor.3. Present an Interactive Artboard:Your UI, built with Gradio or Streamlit, must present all three generated assets (audio, text, and image) together in a clean and cohesive layout. The user should be able to experience the full scene in one place.4. Enable Creative Exploration:Your prototype must allow the user to iteratively refine their creation. At a minimum, include a "Remix" or "Regenerate" button that creates a new version of at least one of the assets, allowing for artistic exploration.5. Document Your Creative Process:Your final submission must be a well-commented Colab notebook that clearly explains your prompts, design choices, and the workflow of your AI art studio.While ambitious ideas like voice synthesis or video generation are exciting, the primary goal within the three-hour constraint is to create a polished core experience that masterfully demonstrates the collaboration between these different AI artists.
